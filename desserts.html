The letters (Y, A, L, L, A, B) use "col-sm-2", giving them a fixed width that keeps them in one row until the screen is very small. In contrast, the dessert names use "col-md" without a set width, allowing them to adjust more easily and wrap to the next line sooner. So, when resizing the window, the dessert names(i.e Apricot Custard Delight) will break first and wrap before the letters do.

Lava Cake

Lava cake is special due to its unique texture, featuring a soft outer layer with a gooey molten center that resembles flowing lava when cut open. Its decadent chocolate flavor delights chocolate lovers, and it offers impressive visual appeal, making it a favorite.

Apricot Delight

Apricot Delight is a chewy sweet treat made primarily from dried apricots, often combined with nuts like almonds or walnuts, and sweetened with sugar or honey. It has a sweet and slightly tangy flavor, and its chewy texture makes it satisfying to eat.

Baklava

Baklava is a rich pastry made from layers of phyllo dough filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with honey or syrup. It features a crunchy, flaky texture with a sweet, sticky filling, often flavored with spices like cinnamon.
